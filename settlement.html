Timsaɛraq Csi To

Ilugan n wurar (Les régles du jeu) :

Deg urar aggi yesɛan werrem (20) temsaɛraq, yal timsaɛraqt tezmar ak desarγah si Yiwet (1) are mraw (10) tneqidin,

ma yella tiririt inek tgarez :

  • lwaqt illa gar seddiset (60) aked semmuset (50) n tisnin : ɣurek mraw (10) tneqiḍin,
  • ma yella lwaqt gar semmuset (50) aked kuzet(40) n tisnin : ɣurek sediss (6) tneqiḍin,
  • ma yella lwaqt gar kuzet (40) aked Kradet (30) n tisnin : ɣurek kuz (4) tneqiḍin,
  • ma yella lwaqt gar kradet (30) aked werrem (20) n tisnin : ɣurek krad (3) tneqiḍin,
  • ma yella lwaqt gar werrem (20) aked mraw (10) n tisnin : ɣurek snat (2) tneqiḍin,
  • ma yella lwaqt gar mraw (10) aked yiwet n (1) tisnit : ɣurek yiwet (1) n tneqiṭ kan

Afud iggarzen.
